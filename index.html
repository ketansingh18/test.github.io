About Kanpur

Garg Aviations Pilot Training Institute

Garg Aviations Limited (GAL) is a flight training academy established in the year 1995 at Civil Airport, Kanpur and engaged in conducting Director General of Civil Aviation, Govt. of India approved flying training courses. The academy has trained numerous students from all over India including candidates from Air force, Navy and Coast guard for obtaining their Private Pilot License/Commercial Pilot License/Airline Transport Pilot License/Instrument rating/ Multi engine rating/Instructor rating etc. The students trained by GAL include self sponsored candidates as well as students sponsored by the government.
